Goal

How Might We create a digital platform that helps employees on parental leave at State Farm return to the workforce with ease?

Purpose

The primary objective of this project is to design and implement a digital platform aimed at assisting State Farm employees who are on parental leave to transition back into the workforce seamlessly. The platform will address key challenges faced by these employees, ensuring they feel supported, informed, and connected throughout their leave and upon their return.

Research

We conducted research by speaking with a few different people who had been on parental leave with State Farm.
